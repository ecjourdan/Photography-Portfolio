Photo Collections

Featured Collection

Great Saltee Island

The Great Saltee Island is a haven for summer visitors like the Atlantic Puffin and Razorbill. The island is also home to a large Gannet colony. Due to the large number of fish eating birds, you may smell the puffins before you see them!

Mountains & Valleys

Ireland may not have the biggest mountains in the world, but the dramatic skies make them appear massive. Every time I return to a location it photographs in a completely new way. This collection features mountains and valleys from Donegal to Kerry.

Mammals of Ireland

Mammals in Ireland can be difficult to find. Many are nocturnal and hide in thick hedgerows. Others hide under the the dark, cold ocean. Still, a few are easier to spot. This collection features red foxes and grey seals in Dublin City, and red deer in Killarney National Park.

Coastlines

Walking along a coastline is one of the best reminders of the power and beauty nature has to offer. This is a collection of some of the most stunning views Ireland has to offer. These photos were taken at Tory Island, West Cork, Howth Head, and Carlingford.

River to Sea

Nestled in the heart of Dublin City is one of the finest examples of biodiversity in Ireland. The River Dodder is an incredible place for wildlife photography. There are regular kingfishers, cormorants, herons, foxes, and even an occasional otter. Eventually, the river runs out to Dublin Bay where terns and black-headed gulls feed.
