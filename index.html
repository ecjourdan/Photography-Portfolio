Wildlife & Landscape Photography

I aim to capture the dynamic light, colour, and movement of the natural world. From the rugged coastlines of west Ireland to the nesting shorebirds of Dublin Bay, I photograph wildlife and their surrounding landscapes. I believe photography is a power medium to share the beauty of nature from remote islands to city rivers. I hope my photography inspires you to respect and protect the nature that surrounds us.

Featured Collection

Great Saltee Island

The Great Saltee Island is a haven for summer visitors like the Atlantic Puffin and Razorbill. The island is also home to a large Gannet colony. Due to the large number of fish eating birds, you may smell the puffins before you see them!
