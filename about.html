About me

I am a professional digital designer and amateur nature photographer. I made this website to show my passion for photography and nature, and to better share my work with others. I started photography shortly after moving to Ireland from the United States several years ago. My favourite aspect of wildlife photography is the ability to observe the facial expressions of animals, and get a tiny glimpse into their lives. My goal is to share the beauty of nature and to inspire others to protect nature. I do not offically sell my photos, however, if you are interested in using any of my photos, please fill out the contact form.
